# Daylio User Reviews

**Research method:** Observational review harvest from App Store, Google Play, r/daylio, r/anxiety, r/bipolar, r/Journaling, r/bulletjournal, productivity forums. Verbatim quotes only. No paraphrasing.

**Key app context:** Daylio is a minimalist mood + activity tracker with a 5-face mood scale and tap-to-log activity icon grid. No writing required. Free tier with premium for unlimited activities, PIN lock, export, and custom moods.

## Summary of patterns

- **Friction is the product.** 30-second entry is the whole moat. Any Lanae adaptation must not add fields to the "lite log" path.
- **5-face scale beats 1-10 for casual logging** because it removes calibration anxiety.
- **Year-in-pixels is emotionally powerful** and trivial to render (365 colored squares).
- **Mood-activity correlation in "top 5 best day vs worst day" format** is more readable than scatter plots.
- **Guilt-free missed days** is non-negotiable. No streak shaming.
- **Multiple entries per day** captures mood volatility that daily-only systems miss.
- **Customization unlocks personal meaning.** Renaming 'Awful' to 'Rough' is a small thing that makes the app feel yours.
