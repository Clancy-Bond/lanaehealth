# Sleep Tracking -- Competitive Analysis

## Oura Ring App (Lanae's Wearable, Gold Standard Hardware)
**LOVE:** Best sleep hardware, discreet ring form, full-night HRV graph (unique), 7-contributor sleep score, polished UI, menstrual cycle integration, Symptom Radar
**HATE:** $5.99/mo subscription to see data YOU collected, sleep stage accuracy only 55-72%, non-traditional schedules penalized, generic early AI advisor
**WISH:** Apple Watch companion, better shift-work support, manual sleep editing, free access to basic scores

## WHOOP (Recovery-Focused, Athletes)
**LOVE:** Dynamic Sleep Need calculation (adjusts for strain + debt), Sleep Consistency metric, Recovery-to-sleep feedback loop, Sleep Coach with bedtime recommendations, polysomnography-validated
**HATE:** Sleep tracking fails when getting up at night, thick wristband uncomfortable, no display, pricing mess, overly athlete-focused
**WISH:** On-device screen, better interrupted sleep handling, general health focus

## Sleep Cycle (No Wearable Needed)
**LOVE:** Smart alarm (wakes during light sleep), no wearable required, snore detection with playback, "Who's Snoring?" partner distinction, 15+ years of data
**HATE:** Lower accuracy than wearables, opaque scoring, phone must be near bed, limited health metrics
**WISH:** Wearable integration for accuracy, better data export

## SleepWatch (Apple Watch AI)
**LOVE:** Truly personalized AI insights correlating daytime behaviors to sleep, automatic tracking, heart rate dip tracking
**HATE:** Subscription, less polished UI, Apple-only, battery drain
**WISH:** Better UI, cross-platform

## AutoSleep (One-Time Purchase Champion)
**LOVE:** No subscription (one-time purchase), clinical-grade data export, customizable sensitivity, depth rivaling subscription apps
**HATE:** Complex UI, steep learning curve, Apple Watch only
**WISH:** Simpler onboarding

## Our Edge
- Pain-sleep bidirectional analysis ("pain was 7/10, deep sleep dropped 40%")
- Flare prediction from sleep pattern changes
- POTS-specific metrics (overnight autonomic stability)
- Menstrual cycle phase overlay on sleep trends
- "Unrefreshing sleep" metric (hallmark of dysautonomia)
- Medication impact before/after comparison
- Trend-first UI to reduce orthosomnia
- No subscription lock on existing Oura data (1,187 days already in DB)
