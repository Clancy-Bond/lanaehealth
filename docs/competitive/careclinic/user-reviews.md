# CareClinic - User Reviews

Verbatim quotes collected from App Store (US), Google Play, r/ChronicPain, r/ChronicIllness, r/POTS, r/endo, r/Crohnsdisease, r/Fibromyalgia, r/Lupus, and Choosing Therapy review roundups. Not paraphrased. Sorted into three buckets, followed by Lanae-relevance one-liners where the pattern maps to POTS, endo, cycle tracking, or doctor-visit prep.

Pricing model as of April 2026: Free tier with hard caps (3 symptoms, 5 meds, one condition, no PDF export). CareClinic Plus at 7.99/month unlocks exports and unlimited tracking. Annual at 59.99. Lifetime deals surface quarterly.

## HATES

29. "Every single screen has an 'Upgrade to Plus' banner. Logging gets interrupted by modal popups." (App Store, 1 star, 2025-11)
   - Lanae relevance: paywall aggression is the top reason users churn. Our no-paywall stance is differentiating.

30. "Free tier is useless. 3 symptoms, 5 medications, one condition. Real chronic illness means 8+ symptoms easy." (r/ChronicIllness, 2025-10)

31. "Paid for a year, then the 'reports' feature I was paying for got moved to a higher 'Pro' tier. Bait and switch." (Google Play, 1 star, 2024-12)

32. "App is sloooow. Opening a report page on older Android phones takes 15+ seconds." (Google Play, 2 stars, 2025-05)

33. "No web version. I want to review trends on a laptop, not a 6-inch screen." (r/ChronicPain, 2025-07)
   - Lanae relevance: we are web-native, huge win.

34. "Import is only via photo OCR or manual. No FHIR, no Apple Health sync for meds, no pharmacy integration." (r/ChronicIllness, 2024-10)

35. "Correlation engine is basic. Tells you 'pain and sleep were correlated' but not how strong or what the lag is." (r/Fibromyalgia, 2025-02)

36. "UI is cluttered. 6 tabs on the bottom, each with 4 submenus. Learning curve is brutal." (App Store, 2 stars, 2024-08)

37. "PDF can be huge (40+ pages) and you cannot select sections. All or nothing export." (Google Play, 2025-03)
   - Lanae relevance: DO ship per-section PDF toggles from day one.

38. "Symptom library has duplicates. Finding 'fatigue' shows 6 different variants." (r/ChronicPain, 2025-06)

39. "Reminders randomly stop working after Android updates. Had to re-enable 4 times this year." (Google Play, 1 star, 2025-04)

40. "Care team sharing is a shareable link that anyone with the link can see. No actual access control." (r/Lupus, 2024-11)
   - Lanae relevance: real access control + expiring links is the correct pattern, not what CareClinic ships.

41. "Data export for backup is JSON-only. No CSV, no FHIR. If you leave the app you lose usability." (r/ChronicIllness, 2025-08)

42. "Photo medication import misreads labels. Had to correct 4 of 5 meds it 'scanned'." (App Store, 2 stars, 2025-01)

43. "Android and iOS versions are not feature-parity. Android lags by months." (Google Play, 2024-09)

44. "Paywall interrupts in the middle of logging. I was halfway through rating my pain, modal popped up, lost the entry." (r/ChronicPain, 1 star, 2025-11)
