# Medication Tracking -- Competitive Analysis

## Medisafe (Was #1, Now Under Siege After Paywall)
**LOVE:** Persistent reminders, Medfriend caregiver alerts, PDF adherence reports, 90+ health trackers, medication education leaflets
**HATE:** 2-medication free cap (Jan 2026 paywall -- devastating for polypharmacy patients, 22.4% of adults 40-79 take 5+), not brain-fog friendly, no pill photo ID, can't skip without reason, limited offline
**WISH:** Unlimited free medications, cognitive-impairment-friendly design, supplement interaction checking

## CareClinic (Chronic Illness Power Tool)
**LOVE:** Medication-symptom correlation engine, PRN with max dose limits, tapering schedules, variable interval scheduling, 24K+ drug database
**HATE:** Overwhelming complexity, steep learning curve, feels like data-entry chore
**WISH:** Simpler default view with progressive disclosure

## Round Health (UX Minimalist, iOS Only)
**LOVE:** One-tap confirmation, beautiful UI, birth control pack flow, reminder windows (flexible timing), doesn't shame for missed doses
**HATE:** iOS only, no interaction checking, no reports, no correlation analysis
**WISH:** Android version, interaction checking, doctor reports

## MyTherapy (Free All-Rounder)
**LOVE:** Completely free, unlimited medications, injection site tracking, monthly reports, recently redesigned UI
**HATE:** No drug interaction checking, no correlation analysis, ad-supported
**WISH:** Interaction checking, deeper analytics

## Theraview (ADHD-Specific, Innovative)
**LOVE:** Medication onset/peak/duration curves (shows when meds kick in and wear off), per-dose effectiveness notes, all data local
**HATE:** Limited to ADHD stimulants, niche
**WISH:** Broader medication coverage

## Key Market Insight
Medisafe's January 2026 paywall created a refugee market of millions of users needing a free alternative. MyTherapy and Pillo are absorbing them, but neither offers chronic illness depth.

## Our Edge
- One-tap dose confirmation (core action = 1 tap)
- PRN as first-class citizen (time since last dose, max-dose warnings, frequency trends)
- Intelligence Engine medication-symptom correlation ("symptom started 3 days after dose change")
- Oura/wearable-correlated adherence (how does med timing affect sleep/HRV?)
- Onset/peak/duration visualization (Theraview-style, for all medications)
- Free unlimited medications
- No streak shame (cumulative %, never resets)
- Brain-fog accessible design
- Cycle-aware scheduling
