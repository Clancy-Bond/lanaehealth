## Signal summary

- Bad-day forgiveness is the single most-mentioned love. No streak-breaking, no pet death, no "you missed X days" messaging.
- Goal scaling based on energy state is a distinguishing feature. Users want the app to adapt to them, not the reverse.
- Micro-task completion (4-tap check-in, water, breathing) drives engagement more than deep journaling.
- Tree of Life history view is emotionally resonant for chronic illness users. Celebration framing beats deficit framing.
- Main complaints center on premium paywalls for aesthetic content and lack of wearable integration. Neither applies to LanaeHealth.
- Cross-cutting wish: data portability to therapists and doctors. This is literally our thesis.
