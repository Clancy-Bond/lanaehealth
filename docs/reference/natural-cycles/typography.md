# Natural Cycles typography

Observed from `docs/reference/natural-cycles/frames/full-tour/frame_{0080,0160}.png`.

## Typeface

A humanist sans with warmer curves than Oura's more geometric stack. Matches the explanatory-surface pairing with SF Pro on iOS.

## Letter spacing

NC uses slightly tighter tracking on display titles ("NC° Birth Control" shows -0.01em) and normal tracking on body.

## Emphasis patterns

- Day numbers inside circles are centered, regular weight : the shape of the circle carries the emphasis, not the type.
- Temperature values on the right side ("96.15°F", "96.89°F") are in a pill-shaped badge with regular weight. The degree symbol is slightly smaller.
- Dates above day rows ("4", "5", "6") are tiny, muted, positioned outside the pill-on-rail.

## Voice cues visible in type

- Headlines tend to ask, not state: "How do you feel today?" vs "Daily log".
- Body paragraphs are short: one idea per paragraph, generous line-height.
- No exclamation marks; optimism is carried by color and shape.
