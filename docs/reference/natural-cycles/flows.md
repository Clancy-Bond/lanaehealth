# Natural Cycles flows

From the 319-frame full tour under `docs/reference/natural-cycles/frames/full-tour/`. NC is our voice and pedagogy reference; this doc is the source for v2 copy patterns.

## Today check-in

A single focused question per screen. The user answers with a single tap and advances. Copy pattern:

- Title: the question (e.g. "How did you sleep?").
- Subtitle: a kind clarifier if needed ("Rough nights happen. No wrong answer.").
- Options: large tappable cards with short labels, never more than five.

## Period log

- Pink hero block indicating the current phase.
- List of flow options (light / medium / heavy / spotting) as big pill buttons.
- Copy: "You're in day 7 of your cycle. Log what you're feeling today."

## History + predictions

- Vertical rail timeline (see `components.md`).
- Each day tappable to show detail below without navigating away.
- Predicted days outlined, not filled; copy clarifies: "This is a prediction, not yet confirmed."

## Voice patterns to reuse in v2

1. **Ask, don't demand.** "How are you feeling?" beats "Log your mood."
2. **Acknowledge uncertainty.** Predictions say "predicted" or "expected," never "will be."
3. **Kind synonyms for clinical terms.**
   - "Rough" not "Bad"
   - "Paused" not "Disabled"
   - "Heads up" not "Warning"
   - "Give it a moment" not "Loading"
4. **Second person, present tense.** "You're in day 7" rather than "User is on day 7."
5. **Never blame.** "We couldn't reach the server. Try again in a moment." vs "Error: server unreachable."

## Copy snippets worth mirroring verbatim

- Empty state: "Nothing logged yet. Tap the plus to add your first entry."
- Confirmation: "Saved. You can always edit or delete later."
- Soft failure: "That didn't go through. Want to try again?"
- Explanatory: "This is a safe place to log what you notice, day by day. It builds the picture your doctor will see."
